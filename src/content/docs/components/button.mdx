Buttons are used to trigger an action or event, such as submitting a form, opening a modal, or serving as a link to another page.

<Aside>
  Button styles are generated via a [Tailwind
  Plugin](https://tailwindcss.com/docs/plugins) which can be found and adapted
  [here](https://github.com/ekfuhrmann/starlight-docs/blob/master/config/tailwind/buttons.js).
</Aside>

## Variants

They come in many different styles such as **contained**, **outlined**, **subtle**, **text**, and **icon**, though styles should be added and removed as necessary based on the needs of the application.
